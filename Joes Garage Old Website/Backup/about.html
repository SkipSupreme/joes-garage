About Joe

Joe Nunn has been doing outdoor bike repair on the Bow River Pathway near downtown since 2007.

  • He's been a bicycle mechanic for over 30 years!
  • He enjoys bringing old clunkers back to life.
  • He gives each bike equal and unrivaled level of care
  • He's familiar with components of all eras.
  • He'd much rather repair a part than replace it. This is beneficial to both clients and the environment.
  • He's happy to share his knowledge of cycling.

Joe enjoys many aspects of cycling. He has:

  • He's raced road and mountain bikes,
  • done multi-day cycle tours,
  • worked as a bike messenger, and
  • used a bicycle as his main mode of urban transportation for many years.
