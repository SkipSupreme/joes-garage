Joe's Garage is a unique business that offers bicycle services from a converted shipping container near the scenic Bow River.

Joe's Garage has a history of mobility and innovation. Before settling in his current setup, a customized 1981 GMC-Grumman Kurbmaster, he ran his shop from a pedal-powered gypsy caravan from 2007 to 2010. His first experiment with a mobile shop was in Toronto in 1996.
