Tune-up Package Prices

We offer three levels of tune-up services for your bike, depending on your needs and budget. All prices are subject to change based on the parts and labour required. Joe will provide you with a quote and get your approval before proceeding with any extra work.

Level 1 - "Make It Safe" - $90

This service includes the basic adjustments and checks to ensure your bike is safe and functional. We will:

  • Inflate tires to optimal pressure
  • Lubricate chain and other moving parts
  • Secure all nuts, bolts and quick releases
  • Fine-tune brakes, gears, hubs and headset
  • Straighten wheels for smooth braking
  • Inspect the whole bike for any damage or wear that may affect safety
  • Test ride the bike and make any final adjustments
  • Charge extra for installing any new parts

Level 2 - "Make It Work" - $150

This service includes everything in Level 1, plus more detailed and precise tuning of your bike's components. We will:

  • Check and tighten all external fittings to the correct torque
  • Optimize gear and brake performance
  • Align disc brake rotors and clean brake pads
  • Lubricate derailleurs and brakes where needed
  • Replace worn or compressed derailleur housing
  • Adjust headset, bottom bracket and hubs for smooth operation
  • Secure cassette and freewheel
  • True and tension wheels in a truing stand with tires mounted
  • Cap all cables, valves and housing for a neat finish
  • Clean rims with steel wool if needed
  • Install any new parts at no extra labour cost

Level 3 - "Make It Nice" - $225

This service includes everything in Level 1 and 2, plus a thorough cleaning and greasing of your bike's parts. We will:

  • Lubricate cables where necessary
  • Grease hubs and lower headset cup where needed
  • True, tension and dish wheels with tires removed
  • Clean frame, fork and wheels with a mild detergent
  • Remove and clean cassette and chain rings
  • Remove, clean and lubricate rear derailleur pulleys
  • Align rear derailleur hanger if necessary
  • Remove cartridge bottom bracket and grease threads where appropriate
  • Offer 50% off on any additional procedures
  • Improve set-up where practical (Make it nice!)
