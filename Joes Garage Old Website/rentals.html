Our Bike Options for Families

If you are looking for a fun and healthy way to enjoy the outdoors with your kids, we have the perfect bikes for you. We offer four different types of bikes that can accommodate children of various ages and abilities:

  • Trail-a-bike: This is a bike that attaches to the back of an adult bike, allowing your child to pedal along with you. It is ideal for kids who are too big for a trailer but not ready to ride on their own.
  • 24” youth bike: This is a bike that is suitable for older kids who can ride independently. It has gears, brakes, and a comfortable seat.
  • 20” child’s bike: This is a bike that is designed for younger kids who are learning to ride.
  • Thule baby trailer: This is a trailer that attaches to the back of an adult bike, allowing you to transport your baby or toddler safely and comfortably.

We have two bikes of each type available for rent. Contact us today to reserve yours and start your family biking adventure!

  • You need to sign a waiver before renting a bike
  • You are responsible for your safety and the safety of others
  • You need to return the bike in good shape
  • You need to return the bike by 6 p.m. or pay for another day.
